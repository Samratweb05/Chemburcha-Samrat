About Shiv Shakti Ganeshutsav Mandal

Chembur cha Samrat

Shiv Shakti Ganeshutsav Mandal, fondly known as Chembur cha Samrat, was established in 2005 with a vision to celebrate the divine presence of Lord Ganesha and bring the community together in the spirit of unity and devotion. Located in the heart of Chembur, a vibrant suburb of Mumbai, our mandal has deep-rooted ties to the rich history and culture of this area. Chembur, a place known for its diverse culture and bustling energy, serves as the perfect backdrop for our celebrations, where people from all walks of life come together to partake in the festivities.

Our mandal draws inspiration from the brave and visionary leadership of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the warrior king who stood for justice, honor, and unity, qualities that we strive to emulate in all our endeavors. His legacy of valor and commitment to the people continues to guide us as we carry forward his ideals through our work and celebrations.

Additionally, the wisdom and influence of Bal Gangadhar Tilak (Lokmanya Tilak), the great freedom fighter and social reformer, play a significant role in our journey. Tilak’s vision of using the Ganesh festival as a platform to unite people and raise social awareness is a tradition that we proudly uphold each year.

Under the leadership of our Adhyaksha, Mahabal G. Shetty, we have grown into a strong community dedicated to the values of unity, service, and devotion. Through our tireless efforts, we continue to bring the community closer and celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi with grandeur, making each year’s festival a memorable one for all.

Join us as we celebrate not just the arrival of Lord Ganesha, but also the spirit of Chembur, Shivaji Maharaj, and Lokmanya Tilak, whose legacies continue to inspire us to serve the people and foster a sense of community pride.
